About Me

My name is Josh Herd, I am a Health, Safety & Environment Manager for a mineral process engineering company living and working in the Salt Lake City, UT area. I completed a Bachelors of Science in Construction Management from Weber State University, and a Master of Science in Occupational Health from the University of Utah.

My end goal with the successful completion of the Coding and Web Development boot camp is to be able to design and build useful and intuitive web applications that can be geared towards solving common workplace health and safety challenges. Whether the goal is to improve a simple equipment inspection, risk assessment, or incident management system that's previously been an inefficient paper based process, being able to take that and build an interactive and efficient web application will be an incredible improvement.
